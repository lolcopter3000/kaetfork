Projects


			I have performed at Electric Picnic, Smock Alley, the Samuel Beckett Theatre, and smaller venues through Ireland. I've worked on more
			than forty theatre shows in the last three years. Those have been mostly with DU Players but also with Trinity's Drama Department, Cork's Granary Theatre,
			and on the Abbey's Peacock Stage. 

			
Music Piece

			 
 Devised theatre piece (single performer with video) created for the Edmund Burke Theatre as part of DU Players' Summerfest 2025
			The music consisted of 55-minutes of music by me. It had nine songs harmonically linked, inspired by the structure of a Quaker Meeting.
			There was no spoken, sung, or read text except for a setting of a Heaney poem. 


			
fish

			 
 Wonderful theatre piece by Laura Pendlebury. Music and recording by me. 
			All text was delivered through recorded voices. Accompanying music made with synths and samples. 
			Though you can't see the puppets, you can hear it as an audio play. 


			
Lego Indiana Jones

			 
 I played bass and guitars on Hope All Well. It has been streamed c. 60,000 times.
